Sicong Pan (潘思聪)

I am doing my Phd research in Humanoid Robots Lab at the University of Bonn under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Maren Bennewitz. I received M.S. degree from the Fudan University under the supervision of Prof. Hui Wei. My research interests lie in Active Perception in tabletop and agricultural scenarios, especially Viewpoint Planning and 3D Object Reconstruction.
