Sicong Pan (潘思聪)

I received M.S. degree from the Fudan University. My research interests lie at the intersection between computer vision and robotics. My master thesis entitled "A Global Adaptive-Multiresolution Next-Best-View Method for Reconstruction of 3D Unknown Objects" was written under the supervision of Prof. Hui Wei.

Research Interests: 3-D reconstruciton, View and path planning, Active vision, Coverage optimization
