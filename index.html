Sicong Pan (潘思聪)

I am doing my Phd research in Humanoid Robots Lab at the University of Bonn under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Maren Bennewitz. I received M.S. degree from the Fudan University. My master thesis entitled "A Global Adaptive-Multiresolution Next-Best-View Method for Reconstruction of 3D Unknown Objects" (in Chinese) was written under the supervision of Prof. Hui Wei. My research interests lie at the intersection between computer vision and robotics, especially for Viewpoint Planning and 3D object Reconstruction using Deep Learning and Coverage Optimization.

Academic Services: RA-L Reviewer, IROS 2023 Reviewer, ICRA 2024 Reviewer
